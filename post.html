E-Com Weekly: Checkout UX wins and Shopify speed tricks

Small fixes that move conversion without heavy dev work.

What moved the needle

  • Clarified error states on checkout forms (less rage clicking).
  • Compressed hero imagery with AVIF fallback.
  • Added “as low as” payment badges above the fold.

Quick Shopify wins

Consider lazy-loading below-the-fold sections and deferring non-critical apps. Measure before/after in GA4 and Shopify.

Next steps

  1. Ship one UX fix per week.
  2. Test a shorter checkout label set.
  3. Audit PDP app bloat.
