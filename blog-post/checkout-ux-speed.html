E‑Com Weekly: Checkout UX wins and Shopify speed tricks

Small fixes that move conversion: clearer error states, trust signals at decision points, and speed wins that don’t wreck UX.

What moved the needle

  • Error states that actually help — put the error next to the field, keep it concise, and autofocus the first invalid input.
  • Trust signals near the CTA — payment badges and a one‑liner on security by the “Complete order” button reduced hesitation.
  • Mobile spacing — bigger taps, 16px minimum label size, and turning off auto‑cap for email fields.

Quick Shopify speed wins

  • Serve hero images as AVIF with WebP fallback; keep product images under ~200KB where possible.
  • Lazy‑load below‑the‑fold sections and defer non‑critical app scripts.
  • Remove duplicate analytics tags and unused app leftovers.

Next steps

  1. Ship a simple progress bar in checkout.
  2. Shorten shipping method labels; cut jargon.
  3. Audit app script load time; eliminate anything over 200ms TBT impact.
